Understanding Off-Track Garage Doors

An off-track garage door occurs when one or both of the door's tracks become misaligned. This misalignment can cause the door to jam, operate unevenly, or become stuck halfway. Common causes of off-track doors include accidental impact, worn or damaged rollers, loose or bent tracks, or problems with the door's cables or springs. It is essential to address an off-track door promptly to prevent further damage and ensure the safety of your garage and its contents.
